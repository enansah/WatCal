How We Protect You

At WatCal, we prioritise the security and privacy of your electricity usage data. Our advanced encryption technology and rigorous data protection protocols are in place to safeguard your consumption information, ensuring confidentiality and peace of mind for all users.
Data Masking
Employ data masking techniques to conceal sensitive information, such as personally identifiable information (PII) and financial details, from unauthorised users. This helps prevent unauthorised access to sensitive data even in the event of a breach.
Encryption
Utilize encryption protocols such as SSL/TLS to encrypt data transmitted between users' devices and the website servers, ensuring that sensitive information remains secure during transmission.
Regular Security Audits
Conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments of the website and its infrastructure to identify and address any potential security vulnerabilities or weaknesses proactively.
User Education and Awareness
Provide educational resources and guidelines to users on best practices for maintaining security, such as creating strong passwords, avoiding phishing scams, and recognising suspicious activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Still you have any questions? Contact our Team via support@WatCal.com
How does the billing sharing website work?
Our billing sharing website allows landlords and tenants to accurately track and allocate electricity costs based on individual consumption. Landlords can create billing groups and add properties, while tenants can view their usage and make payments through their accounts.
How do I add my property to the website for billing sharing?
Landlords can easily add properties to the website by providing property details such as address, unit numbers, and meter readings.
How often are electricity bills generated and distributed?
Electricity bills are typically generated on a monthly basis and distributed to tenants electronically through their accounts on the website.
Can I customize the way electricity costs are allocated among tenants?
Yes, landlords have the flexibility to customize how electricity costs are allocated among tenants based on various factors such as square footage, number of occupants, or usage patterns.
What should I do if I encounter technical issues with the website?
If you encounter any technical issues, please contact our customer support team for assistance. We're here to help resolve any issues you may encounter.
How does the website handle privacy and data protection?
We take privacy and data protection seriously and adhere to strict security measures to safeguard your personal information. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for detailed information on how we handle your data.
